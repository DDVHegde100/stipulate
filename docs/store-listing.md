## Full description

Stipulate is your card benefit co-pilot. Add cards to your wallet, route any purchase to the card that earns the most, and get alerted when issuer benefits change.

**Features**
- Wallet with 200+ US cards
- Sub-20ms routing by merchant and category
- Benefit change alerts (email + push)
- Receipt OCR for merchant extraction
- Batch route for weekly spend planning

Parse the stipulations. Route the payment.
